-Pegs
	-Drag from palette colors to create pegs.
	-Point of peg on mouse cursor
	-Release drops peg; if mouse hovers over hole, peg is added to hole
	-If there are free floating pegs and you drag another, floater is deleted
	-right click to delete
	-dropping peg on palette will also delete
	-can drag from hole as well, and r-click will also delete (makes it easier in freeplay, so might as well)
	-Click palette color to select
    	-Click empty hole to fill with that color peg
    	-Click peg (or hole w/ peg) to change that peg's color
